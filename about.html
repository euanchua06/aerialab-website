About AeriaLab

Empowering Singapore's Agricultural Future

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by a group of students at Singapore Polytechnic, AeriaLab was born from a simple observation: Singapore's heavy reliance on food imports presents both a challenge and an opportunity. With the nation's ambitious "30 by 30" goal—to produce 30% of nutritional needs locally by 2030—we recognized the urgent need for innovative solutions to support our local farms.

What started as a student project has evolved into a mission to transform how agriculture operates in Singapore and beyond.

The Challenge

Vertical farms across Singapore face mounting pressures. Rising operational costs are squeezing profit margins, making it increasingly difficult for smaller farms to stay competitive. Manual labor costs continue to climb, while the need for skilled and experienced farmers creates a significant barrier to entry and scalability.

For many small to medium-sized operations, these challenges threaten their very survival. Traditional monitoring methods are time-intensive, require expert knowledge, and often lack the consistency needed for optimal crop management.

Our Solution

The Harvester II Hand-held Scanner represents our answer to these challenges. We believe that advanced agricultural technology shouldn't be a luxury reserved for large commercial operations. Our scanner is designed to be:

  • Affordable: Priced to be accessible to smaller farms without compromising on capability
  • User-Friendly: No extensive training or agricultural expertise required—intuitive design that anyone can use
  • Accurate: Computer vision technology powered by YOLO models ensures consistent, reliable plant health assessment
  • Efficient: Dramatically reduces time spent on manual inspection while improving data quality

By combining Intel RealSense depth sensing, NVIDIA Jetson processing power, and our custom plant detection algorithms, we've created a tool that empowers farmers to make data-driven decisions without breaking the bank.

Who We Serve

Our primary focus is supporting farms across Singapore—from small urban vertical operations to larger agricultural facilities. We understand that each farm faces unique challenges, which is why our solution is designed to adapt to different scales and types of operations.

Whether you're running a rooftop garden, a container farm, or a commercial greenhouse, Harvester II provides the insights you need to optimize your growing operations.

Looking Ahead

While we started with agriculture, our vision extends far beyond farms. The technology behind Harvester II—computer vision, portable hardware, and intuitive data management—has applications across multiple industries. We're actively exploring how our platform can address challenges in inventory management, quality control, environmental monitoring, and more.

As we grow, our commitment remains the same: developing affordable, accessible technology that solves real-world problems.

Our Mission

To democratize advanced monitoring technology, making precision agriculture accessible to farms of all sizes while supporting Singapore's journey toward food security.
